Are you feeling low?Come to me.
Are you living in depression?Come to me.
Has your sky is stolen?Come to me.
Have your sleepless nights filled with \xe2\x80\x9cWhy me?\xe2\x80\x9d killer question?Come to me.
Your dreams are shattered? Come to me.
Who am I?I am living inside you,I have four rooms to accommodate all your fearsall your tearsall your painall your rejections
